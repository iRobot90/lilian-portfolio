About Lilian

A Vision of
Grace & Purpose

Lilian Shihundu Opete is a 28-year-old Kenyan model, pageant queen and PWDS advocate whose presence commands every runway and stage she graces. Based in Mombasa, she brings a rare combination of elegance, strength and purpose to the fashion world.

A trailblazer for inclusion and diversity, Lilian has competed and won at national and international levels — representing Kenya with distinction across East Africa and beyond. She uses fashion as a powerful platform for positive societal change.
